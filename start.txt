What Is Python?

What Is Data Engineering?
Data Engineering is the process of collecting & storing datasets of 
varying formats and maintaining this data in a relational database for
further interpretation and analysis by Data Scientist, product managers 
as well as other senior members of staff.

What Is an Interpreter
The "translater" of your system which allows the conversion of high
level programming languages to low level machine code.
